In Quarentenado, you take on the role of André, a boy who, faced with a pandemic situation, is forced to take care of the housework while his mother is at work, since she has not been released. Take care that the protagonist does not reset his health and fun bars. Interact with the objects of the house and do the obligations to survive the Quarantine! Download!

❮ ❯

The game Quarentenado was developed during the CoronaJam , a GameJam promoted by developer and youtuber Amdré Young.
